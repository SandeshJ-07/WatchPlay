F.R.I.E.N.D.S

1994 | 13+ | 10 Seasons | Sitcoms

This hit sitcom follows the merry misadventures of six 20-something pals as they navigate the pitfalls of work, life and love in 1990s Manhattan.

Starring:
Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry, David Schwimmer
Creators:
David Crane, Marta Kauffman

Actors

David Schwimmer
David Lawrence Schwimmer is an American actor, comedian, director and producer. Schwimmer began his acting career performing in school plays at Beverly Hills High School.

1. The One Where Monica Gets a Roommate (Pilot)

Rachel runs from her wedding and meets the friends in the coffee place. Ross is depressed about his divorce but he still has a crush on Rachel.

2. The One with the Sonogram at the End

Ross's lesbian ex-wife is pregnant with his child, and he doesn't like her choice of a last name for the baby.

3. The One with the Thumb

A soda company gives Phoebe $7,000 after she finds a dismembered thumb in a can of soda.

4. The One with George Stephanopoulos

A deliveryman accidentally brings a pizza meant for George Stephanopoulos, who lives across the street from the girls.

5. The One with the East German Laundry Detergent

Ross helps Rachel do laundry and considers the evening a first date. Joey has Monica pose as his new girlfriend.

6. The One with the Butt

Joey gets his big film break when he's hired to be Al Pacino's stunt butt.

7. The One with the Blackout

During a citywide blackout, Ross tries to tell Rachel that he likes her.

8. The One Where Nana Dies Twice

Monica and Ross mourn the death of their grandmother. Chandler questions his sexuality.

9. The One Where Underdog Gets Away

Monica's first Thanksgiving dinner for the gang burns when they all run to the rooftop to see the runaway Underdog balloon from the parade.

10. The One with the Monkey

The gang makes and then breaks a pact to celebrate New Year's Eve without dates. Lonely Ross gets a roommate: a monkey named Marcel.

11. The One with Mrs. Bing

When Chandler's romance novelist mother comes to New York to visit him, Joey catches her and Ross kissing.

12. The One with the Dozen Lasagnas

Monica gives Paolo one of the dozen lasagnas she made for her aunt, who doesn't want them anymore because they've got meat in them.
